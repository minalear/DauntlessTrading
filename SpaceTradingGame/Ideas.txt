When it comes to navigating the galaxy, players can calculate routes based off of various circumstances.
  * the quality of their engine
    * engines will have a "jump radius" spec that let players jump a set distance with a set amount of fuel
	* may allow engines to jump out of that radius, but at the expense of a lot more fuel
Functional stock market listing all businesses
  * stealing and attacking shipments from these businesses will cause stock to drop
  * increasing business will raise stock prices
Gathering favor with different factions will result in better trade deals
  * They will accept lower margins on trades and may even accept negative trades
Character creation and galaxy creation are separate, allowing players to play different people similar to Dwarf Fortress Adventure Mode
